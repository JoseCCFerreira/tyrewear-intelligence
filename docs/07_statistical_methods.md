# 07. Statistical Methods

Statistical tests help decide whether differences are meaningful.

Methods included:

- Shapiro-Wilk and Kolmogorov-Smirnov for normality
- Levene for variance equality
- t-test and Welch t-test for two-group mean comparison
- ANOVA for multiple-group mean comparison
- Mann-Whitney and Kruskal-Wallis for non-parametric comparison
- Chi-square for categorical association
- Pearson and Spearman for correlation

p-value below 0.05 is evidence against the null hypothesis, not absolute proof.
